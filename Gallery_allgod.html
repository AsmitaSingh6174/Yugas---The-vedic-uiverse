Lord Ganesha
Lord Ganesha, remover of obstacles, god of wisdom and beginnings.
Lord Hanuman
Lord Hanuman, mighty devotee of Rama, symbolizes strength, devotion, courage.
Maa Saraswati
Maa Saraswati, goddess of wisdom, music, arts, learning, and knowledge.
Maa PArvati
Maa Parvati, divine mother, goddess of power, love, and devotion.
Maa Laxmi
Maa Lakshmi, goddess of wealth, prosperity, fortune, abundance, and blessings.
Suryadev
Suryadev, sun god, source of light, energy, power, and life.
Vayu Dev
Vayudev, wind god, controller of air, life force, and breath.
YamRaj
Yamraj, god of death, justice, and dharma, guides departed souls.
Bhudevi
Bhudevi, earth goddess, sustainer of life, fertility, stability, and prosperity.
Lord Shiva
Lord Shiva, the destroyer, meditator, supreme yogi, symbolizes transformation and power.
Mata Sita
Maa Sita, goddess of virtue, devotion, patience, purity, and strength.
Varun Dev
Varundev, god of oceans, water, cosmic order, justice, and truth.
Agni Dev
Agnidev, fire god, purifier, messenger, sustainer of rituals and energy.
Lord Brahma
Lord Brahma, the creator, four-faced deity, grants wisdom and knowledge.
Lord Kartike
Kartikeya, war god, commander of devas, symbolizes courage, victory, and wisdom.
