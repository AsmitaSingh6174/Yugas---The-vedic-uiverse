Rig Veda

The oldest and most important Veda, composed of hymns (suktas) dedicated to various deities like Agni, Indra, and Varuna. It is a collection of praises, prayers, and philosophical insights about the cosmos and existence.

Sama Veda

The Veda of melodies and chants, mainly derived from the Rigveda but set to music. It is considered the root of Indian classical music and is used in devotional singing and rituals.
