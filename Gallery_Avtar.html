Matasya Avatar
The first incarnation in which Vishnu took the form of a fish.
Kurma Avatar
The second incarnation in which Vishnu took the form of a tortoise.
Varaha Avatar
The third incarnation, in which Vishnu took the form of a pig
Narshimha Avatar
The fourth incarnation, in which Vishnu took the form of half man and half lion.
Vamana Avatar
The fifth incarnation, in which Vishnu took the form of a dwarf Brahmin.
Prarashurama Avatar
The sixth incarnation, in which Vishnu took the form of the sage Parashurama.
Ram Avatar
The seventh incarnation, in which Vishnu took the form of Rama.
Krishna Avatar
The eighth incarnation, in which Vishnu took the form of Krishna.
Buddha Avatar
The ninth incarnation, in which Vishnu took the form of Buddha.
Kalki Avatar
The tenth and final incarnation, who will come at the end of Kaliyuga.
